Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git allows us to work collaboratively and divide tasks between team
members in a effortless manner. 

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Finance, especially with market trend predictions.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging protects the unedited history of the codebase versus rebasing 
stores changes chronologically and on top of the branch.
